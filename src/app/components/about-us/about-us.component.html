Our Story

Founded in 2023, Safarny was born from a passion for simplifying travel for Egyptians. We recognized the challenges travelers face when booking transportation within our beautiful country and set out to create a seamless platform that connects passengers with diverse transportation options.

What started as a small idea has grown into Egypt's preferred travel booking platform, serving thousands of satisfied customers each month and partnering with the country's leading transportation providers.

Our Mission

At Safarny, our mission is to revolutionize how Egyptians travel by providing a user-friendly platform that offers reliable, affordable, and convenient transportation solutions. We strive to:
